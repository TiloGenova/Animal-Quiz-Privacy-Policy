Privacy Policy – My Animals!

Effective Date: February 19, 2026
Developer: Weblion Genova  |  Contact: webliongenova@gmail.com

This Privacy Policy applies to the mobile application "My Animals!" (hereinafter "the App"), developed and operated by Tilo Oschatz.

1. Data Collection and Use

The App does not collect, store, process, transmit, or share any personal data or sensitive user data of any kind.

No data of the following types is collected or accessed:

  • Name, email address, phone number, or other identifying information
  • Location data
  • Device or advertising identifiers
  • Photos, contacts, microphone, camera, or sensor data
  • Usage behavior, diagnostic data, crash logs, or analytics data
  • Any other information that could be used to identify an individual

The App operates entirely offline and locally on the user's device. No data is transmitted to the developer or any third parties at any time.

2. Third Parties

The App does not integrate any third-party SDKs, advertising networks (e.g., AdMob), analytics services (e.g., Firebase Analytics, Google Analytics), crash-reporting tools, or other services that could collect data.

There are no data transmissions to third parties.

3. Permissions

The App requests no sensitive or unnecessary permissions (such as location, contacts, camera, microphone, or broad storage access beyond the app's own sandbox). Any permissions used are strictly for core app functionality and are not used to collect or transmit data.

4. Data Storage and Deletion

Since no personal data is collected, there is no storage or deletion of such data. Any local app data (e.g., user preferences, progress, or settings saved on the device) remains exclusively on the user's device and can be removed at any time by uninstalling the App.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

If, in the future, any changes occur regarding data practices (which is not currently planned), we will update this Privacy Policy and revise the effective date above. We recommend checking this page periodically.

6. Your Rights

As no personal data is processed, no rights regarding access, rectification, deletion, or objection apply to personal data in connection with this App.

If you have any questions, feel free to contact us at webliongenova@gmail.com.

7. Governing Law
